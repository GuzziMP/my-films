
Welcome to MyFilms !


The My Films plugin requires only a minimal setup to work.

Following installation MyFilms Setup will be launched to allow you to configure the plugin.

Although there are many options, most have been predefined for you, with presets. You only need to enter the required information for a few fields (yellow fields) to begin using My Films.
Tool Tips are available for all fields in Setup to assist you. 

Please refer to our extensive MyFilms Wiki to learn all about the new features, advantages and ways to customize MyFilms to your preferences.
A link to the Wiki appears on the bottom in MyFilms Setup and is visible on every page.

Enjoy!
